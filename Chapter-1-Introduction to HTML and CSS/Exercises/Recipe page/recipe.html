Chocolate chip cookies!

These cookies are soft, chewy, and packed with melty chocolate chips. A perfect treat for every occasion!





Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dry Ingredients: In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  2. Mix the Wet Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk the melted butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until smooth.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, whisking well after each. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Combine Wet & Dry: Gradually mix in dry ingredients. Fold in chocolate chips and nuts (if using).

  9. Sprinkle with sea salt (optional), serve warm or store for up to 1 week.

*Tips for the Best Cookies*

  • Melted butter = chewier cookies, softened butter = fluffier cookies.
  • Use high-quality chocolate for rich flavor.
  • Remove slightly underbaked for gooey centers.
  • Bake longer (about 14 min) for crispier cookies.
