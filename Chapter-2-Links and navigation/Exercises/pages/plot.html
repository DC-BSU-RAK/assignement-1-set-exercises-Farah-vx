key plot points

  • Dr. Baek's introduction: Dr. Baek is appointed as the head of the trauma unit at hnuh, where the existing team comprises on-call doctors from other departments. his arrival disrupts the status quo, leading to initial conflicts with colleagues

  • Team formation: recognizing the need for a dedicated trauma team, dr. baek recruits individuals like yang jae-won, a top medical graduate and the only son of a prominent doctor family, who becomes his first disciple. he also enlists nurse cheon jang-mi, a dedicated nurse, and park kyung-won, an anesthesiology resident, forming a cohesive unit

  • Operational challenges: the team faces numerous life-and-death situations, including a catastrophic bridge accident that overwhelms the emergency ward. despite limited resources, they work tirelessly to save lives, with dr. baek performing simultaneous critical surgeries

  • Systemic struggles: operating a high-functioning trauma center strains the hospital's finances, leading to conflicts with administrators who prioritize profits over patient care. dr. baek's relentless pursuit of excellence often clashes with the hospital's financial constraints

  • Personal growth: throughout their journey, the team members confront personal and professional challenges, including dr. yang's initial hesitation in critical situations and nurse cheon's unwavering dedication to patient care. their experiences in the trauma unit test their resilience and commitment to saving lives

The series combines intense medical scenarios with action-adventure elements, highlighting the challenges of establishing a top-tier trauma center within a complex healthcare system. it delves into themes of courage, dedication, and the unbreakable bonds formed in the pursuit of saving others
